Design Notes — Team-Sectioned Mini Cards (Hybrid)

Pros

  • Team-grouped for clarity — each lead knows exactly which cards belong to them
  • Visual mini cards stay scannable from arm's length on a clipboard
  • One page per day fits 15-20 bundles comfortably in landscape A4
  • Color-coded sections distinguish PM vs defect work at a glance
  • Hours totals per team help supervisors balance workload mid-shift
  • Material codes in mono font remain legible even at 5.8pt

Cons / Trade-offs

  • Less write-in space than a pure card layout — notes line is single-line only
  • Spacing gets tight on very busy days (20+ bundles) — may need overflow page
  • Tiny sections (1-2 cards) leave visible empty grid cells unless filled
  • Worker-pill list in the header truncates if a team has 5+ named workers
  • Defect descriptions compete with material pills for vertical space
