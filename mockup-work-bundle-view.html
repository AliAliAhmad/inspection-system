📐 Design Notes
  • 1 Within a PM bundle, mechanical and electrical work splits between sub-teams — they work in parallel on the same equipment, each lead managing their own roster, jobs, and materials.
  • 2 All defects on an equipment that has a PM scheduled get bundled with the PM. The same PM team handles both, with mech defects routed to the Mech sub-team and elec defects routed to the Elec sub-team.
  • 3 Equipment with no PM but defects → handled by the separate Defect Team in a Defect Bundle. If both mech and elec defects exist, the Defect Team also splits into Mech and Elec sub-teams.
